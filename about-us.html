Born from passion, built with purpose.

What began as one woman’s search for purpose has become a growing community of support for mothers and families. Your Maternity Journey was born from a deep passion for helping women feel safe, informed, and empowered from pregnancy to postpartum. Today, we are proud to have a full team of professionals ready to walk this journey with you—with empathy, knowledge, and dedication.

Natalia Pitkowski

CEO, Labor & Postpartum Doula, Infant Care Specialist and Childbirth Educator. CPR and Spinning Baby Certified.

Helping and serving people are my love languages – it is part of who I am.

One day, someone said to me that we are portals during childbirth, that changed my life. I connected deeply with this truth; being a doula started to make sense. That’s when Your Maternity Journey was born and I found my purpose in life: Help birthing a new generation, with love and respect.
