SERVICES

Whether you're preparing for childbirth or navigating the first weeks with your baby, we offer personalized support every step of the way. Explore our services and discover how we can make your maternity journey more peaceful, empowering, and memorable.

This is your pregnancy, your birth and your motherhood. Your Maternity Journey. As doulas, we support and create a non-judgmental environment. We help process complex emotions during pregnancy, as well as offer physical & emotional support during labor & postpartum. As photographers, we record moments full of love and affection. Photography has an incredible power to transport us in time. We are here to save those beautiful memories, from gender reveal to newborn sessions, and beyond.

Families in Philadelphia, PA can now count on Your Maternity Journey! We’re proud to expand our care beyond NY, NJ, and CT — bringing trusted support closer to you.

Placenta encapsulation is a natural way to support your postpartum recovery. Rich in nutrients, the placenta is gently processed into capsules that may help boost energy, stabilize mood, and speed up healing. A tradition with modern benefits, safely handled by trained professionals.
