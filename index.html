ABOUT US

Natalia found her true calling in birthwork and, in 2020, became a certified birth and postpartum doula, as well as a childbirth educator. Driven by a deep passion for supporting and empowering women during one of life’s most transformative experiences, she began offering personalized and compassionate care to families throughout pregnancy, birth, and postpartum. What began as a personal mission has since grown into a thriving agency — Your Maternity Journey — proudly serving over 200 families across New York, New Jersey, Connecticut, and Philadelphia, PA, with the support of a growing team of more than 20 dedicated professionals.

OUR PURPOSE

We are here to guide, support, and uplift you through every phase of your maternity journey. Our mission is to offer not just services, but a community of care, trust, and empowerment for every family we serve.

SERVICES

At Your Maternity Journey, we believe that every stage of motherhood deserves care, respect, and professional support. That’s why we offer a complete range of services designed to guide families from pregnancy through the postpartum period and beyond.

Our team of over 20 dedicated professionals provides continuous labor support, postpartum assistance, and educational courses to help parents feel informed and empowered.

We also offer specialized care such as lactation consulting, prenatal and postpartum massages, and breast laser treatment for breastfeeding-related discomfort.

To honor each birth story, we provide professional photography services that capture the magic of pregnancy, the strength of birth, and the tenderness of the newborn stage.

For those seeking holistic recovery, we offer placenta encapsulation as a natural method of postpartum healing. With warmth, expertise, and cultural sensitivity, we support families in New York, New Jersey, and Connecticut, helping each one navigate their maternity journey with confidence and connection.

In addition to serving families in New York, New Jersey, and Connecticut, Your Maternity Journey is now proud to offer full services in Philadelphia, PA. Our team is ready to support you with the same care, warmth, and professionalism — wherever your journey begins.

SUPPORT OUR DONATION CENTER

Our donation initiative started after we discovered a touching story online about a pregnant mother facing eviction and lacking resources for her baby. Moved by her situation, we rallied our clients to gather essential items for her. Seeing her gratitude inspired us to establish a donation center.

The center is a space where anyone can contribute baby care, pregnancy, and childbirth products. We will distribute these donations to families in need, ensuring they receive the support they require. With community support, we aim to make a significant difference in the lives of mothers and their families.
