ABOUT US

After years working as a nanny in the U.S., Natália found her true calling in birthwork. In 2020, she became a certified birth and postpartum doula, as well as a childbirth educator. Her passion quickly grew into a thriving agency, Your Maternity Journey, now serving over 200 families across New York, New Jersey, and Connecticut with a team of over 20 professionals.

OUR PURPOSE

We are here to guide, support, and uplift you through every phase of your maternity journey. Our mission is to offer not just services, but a community of care, trust, and empowerment for every family we serve.

SERVICES

At Your Maternity Journey, we believe that every stage of motherhood deserves care, respect, and professional support. That’s why we offer a complete range of services designed to guide families from pregnancy through the postpartum period and beyond. Our team of over 20 dedicated professionals provides continuous labor support, postpartum assistance, and educational courses to help parents feel informed and empowered. We also offer specialized care such as lactation consulting, prenatal and postpartum massages, and breast laser treatment for breastfeeding-related discomfort. To honor each birth story, we provide professional photography services that capture the magic of pregnancy, the strength of birth, and the tenderness of the newborn stage. For those seeking holistic recovery, we offer placenta encapsulation as a natural method of postpartum healing. With warmth, expertise, and cultural sensitivity, we support families in New York, New Jersey, and Connecticut, helping each one navigate their maternity journey with confidence and connection.

SUPPORT OUR DONATION CENTER

Our donation initiative started after we discovered a touching story online about a pregnant mother facing eviction and lacking resources for her baby. Moved by her situation, we rallied our clients to gather essential items for her. Seeing her gratitude inspired us to establish a donation center.

This center will be a space where anyone can contribute baby care, pregnancy, and childbirth products. We will distribute these donations to families in need, ensuring they receive the support they require. With community support, we aim to make a significant difference in the lives of mothers and their families.
